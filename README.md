UniHub: IIUC Forum & Store
UniHub is a comprehensive, integrated web platform designed to foster community and resource sharing within the International Islamic University Chittagong (IIUC). It seamlessly combines a Campus Forum for academic discussions and a Marketplace for buying and selling academic resources, all under one roof.

üöÄ Features

For Students
Campus Forum:

Create, edit, and delete posts.

Comment on and like posts to engage in discussions.

View personal activity history (own posts, comments, and likes).

Academic Marketplace:

Browse, search, and filter products (textbooks, stationery, etc.).

Add items to a cart and complete purchases.

Function as a Seller to list, manage, and sell products.

User Dashboard: Manage profile, view order history, and track forum activity.

For Sellers (A Student Role)
Product Management: List new products with details and images, and edit or delete existing listings.

Order Management: View orders and update their status (pending/completed).

Communication: Receive and respond to messages from potential buyers.

For Administrators
User Management: View and manage all registered users and sellers.

Content Moderation: Monitor and delete inappropriate forum posts and comments.

Store Oversight: Manage product listings and monitor transactions for policy compliance.
